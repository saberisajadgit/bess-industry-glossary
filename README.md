# BESS Industry Glossary

A reference glossary for the acronyms and core concepts that come up constantly in Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) engineering, commercial, and procurement work — grid operators, capacity-market accreditation, battery technical terms, fire-safety standards, contract/commercial vocabulary, and revenue-stream terminology.

Built as a companion to **[The Industrial Ledger](#)**, a LinkedIn series on BESS engineering and commercial fundamentals for engineers moving into solutions/sales/application engineering roles. If you're studying for an SE/AE interview, ramping up on a new BESS project, or just tired of Googling the same ten acronyms, this is meant to be a fast, organized reference.

👉 **[Read the full glossary](GLOSSARY.md)**

## Why categories, not alphabetical order

Most real conversations in this industry mix terms from several domains in a single sentence (e.g., "the **IE** checks whether the **ESSA**'s **COD** definition exposes the **LD** clock to **EPC** delay risk"). Grouping related terms together — grid operators, capacity-market math, hardware, safety codes, contracts, revenue — makes the relationships between terms clearer than an A-Z list would, and tends to stick better.

## A note on currency

Capacity-market rules (especially ELCC methodologies) and US tax/trade policy (especially FEOC and ITC eligibility) change frequently. This glossary reflects public standards and reporting as of mid-2026 — always verify against the relevant ISO/RTO's own manuals or current federal guidance before relying on it for anything contractual or compliance-related.
